Dr. Bill

Dr Bill is voice system that acts as an interview prep coach designed to help students practice for software engineering interviews.

My Responsibilities

Design

For this project I worked as a part of a team of three.

What I did

  • Research
  • Prototyping
  • Design


Brainstorming & Research

We chose to design a voice interview application because Tech students are highly career driven. The entire experience of college is to prepare you to get a job. This is also an experience that relies heavily on speaking that students have difficulty with.

The team then conducted a contextual inquiry to learn more about what the current interview experience is like for students. We interviewed twelve students who were either studying Computational Media or Computer Science at Georgia Tech.

We synthesized the data into an affinity diagram which we used to create our identity model.
