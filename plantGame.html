Plant Game

A game about trying to keep your plants alive!

Collect plants and try to keep them alive! Take care of your plants by attending to their needs. Water your plants when they get thirsty and spray some pesticide on them when they have bugs! But be careful! Consistently using the wrong thing can kill plants and not attending to the plant’s needs in time can kill them too! Mature plants can propagate to make more plants which you can sell for money. Money can be used to grab new plants and use certain resources.


This game was built using Godot Engine and programmed using gdScript, a custom scripting language similar to Python for the Godot Engine! All art was drawn by me.

The background music used is Go to the Picnic.



How to Play

  • Use your mouse to click and drag items!
  • Right click a plant to see options to sell or propogate the plant
  • Left click a dead plant (brown) to remove it
  • Water thirsty plants (yellow plants) with the watering can!
  • Remove bugs from plants with the insecticide (Bug-be-gone)
  • Use the fertilizer to reduce the time for a plant to mature or be propogation ready
  • Click on the shop icon to enter the shop, where you can buy more plants
  • Click on the book icon to view the plant book, where you can see your plant records


Known Issues

  • Fertilizer can be a bit wonky
  • If you don’t have money or no room to add plants, the game won’t buy/add the items, but it won’t warn you either
  • The fx for when tools are used will also occur if tools overlap each other
  • Music does not loop
